Lankan Medical Portal has provided online medical advice during the COVID-19 pandemic. Patients can request appointments, view test results, and communicate with their doctors online. The portal has helped integrate healthcare into current care and promote effective patient-clinician encounters and communication. The portal has been especially useful in Sri Lanka, where dialects retain many words and grammatical forms that are not in everyday use in India. The portal has helped patients receive medical advice and care during a difficult time.
